1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal and Sensitive User Data

Dancetopia is designed to provide a dance rhythm game experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and only collect data necessary for app functionality.

Data Type Purpose Collection Method
Audio Files Music playback and beat detection for gameplay User-selected files from device storage
Game Progress Save user scores and progress Local storage on device
App Usage Data Improve app performance and user experience Automatically collected during app usage
Device Information App compatibility and performance optimization Automatically collected

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone data (beyond music files you select)
  • Contacts or phonebook information
  • SMS or call data
  • Health data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Persistent device identifiers (IMEI, IMSI, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Purpose: All data collection is limited to providing core app functionality and improving user experience within Dancetopia.

2.1 Specific Uses

  • Audio Files: Used exclusively for music playback and beat analysis during gameplay
  • Game Progress: Stored locally to maintain your scores and unlocked content
  • Usage Analytics: Help us identify and fix bugs, improve performance
  • Device Information: Ensure app compatibility across different devices

2.2 Data Processing Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: When you grant permissions for file access
  • Legitimate Interest: For app functionality and improvement
  • Contract Performance: To provide the game service you requested

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for monetary consideration.

3.1 Limited Sharing

We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Technical service providers who assist with app functionality (under strict confidentiality)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental request
  • Safety: To protect rights, property, or safety of users or others
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger or acquisition (with adequate notice to users)

3.2 Third-Party SDKs

Dancetopia may include third-party code libraries (SDKs) for essential functionality. All third-party integrations are required to comply with our privacy standards and cannot sell your personal data.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use modern cryptography (HTTPS)
  • Local Storage: Game data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Reviews: Ongoing assessment of security practices

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 User Controls

  • File Access: You can revoke file permissions through device settings
  • Data Deletion: Clear app data through device settings
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

5.2 Account Deletion

If Dancetopia introduces user accounts in future versions, you will have the right to:

  • Request account deletion through the app
  • Request account deletion via our website
  • Have all associated user data deleted within 30 days

5.3 EU/EEA and UK User Rights

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR/UK GDPR:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Retention Policy

  • Game Progress: Stored until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data retained for up to 2 years for improvement purposes
  • Support Requests: Communication records retained for up to 1 year

6.2 Automated Deletion

Most data is automatically deleted when you uninstall Dancetopia from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Dancetopia is designed to be appropriate for all ages, including children. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • No third-party advertising or tracking in areas accessible to children
  • All SDKs used are approved for child-directed services
  • Parents can contact us to review, modify, or delete their child's information

8. International Data Transfers

If we transfer data internationally, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework compliance
  • Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
